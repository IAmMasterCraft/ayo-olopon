How to play (local two-player)

  • Each player owns the six pits on their side.
  • On your turn, pick a pit on your side and sow seeds counterclockwise.
  • If your last seed lands in an opponent pit making exactly 4, capture those 4.
  • Continue capturing backward on the opponent side while pits are exactly 4.
  • The game ends when one side is empty; remaining seeds are collected.

This is a simplified ruleset with chain captures and no starvation rule.
